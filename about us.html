ABOUT US

Lilly & Jerry Day Care was founded in 2017 (NPO NO. 187-743) with a deep love for children and a passion for early childhood development. With years of combined experience, our founders and staff are committed to creating a safe, engaging, and nurturing environment for every child. Our approach blends learning through play, structured curriculum, and hands-on exploration - ensuring that each child thrives emotionally, intellectually, and socially. We are more than a daycare - we are a community of caregivers, educators, and families working together to provide children with the best possible start in life. Every day, we create opportunities for growth, joy, and discovery, knowing that the early years shape who children will become.

Every day at our daycare is filled with joyful moments - children painting with focus, laughing together during storytime, or exploring the world around them through outdoor play. These experiences not only support their learning but help them build friendships, confidence, and curiosity.

We believe in being actively present with the children. Our supervisor and caregivers are not just watching - they're involved in the activities, guiding play, supporting emotions, and sharing in each child's small but meaningful victories.

We are proud to share our story and introduce you to the people behind Lilly & Jerry Day Care. Our team is more than just educators; we are caregivers, mentors, and trusted partners in your child's early childhood education extends beyond the classroom - it's reflected in every interaction, every plan we make, and every milestone your child's achieves. Thank you for getting to know us. We are excited about the opportunity to welcome your family into our community, and we are confident that together, we can create a joyful, supportive, and enriching experience for your child. Your trust means everything to us.
