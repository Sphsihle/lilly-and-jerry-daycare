PROGRAMS & SERVICES

We provide a nurturing environment with a structured educational program, ensuring the growth, learning, and development of every child in our care. Through age appropriate activities like storytelling, arts and crafts, music and movement, and outdoor play, we encourage creativity, physical development, and strong social skills in all our children. Our programs are carefully tailored to meet children at their individual stages of development, allowing them to explore, express, and discover at their own pace. Whether building literacy, learning to share, or to navigating their emotions. Our children receive the guidance and support they need to thrive both in the classroom and beyond.

We invite you to take a deeper look into the programs and services, we offer at Lilly & Jerry Day Care . Each activity, routine, and lesson is purposefully designed to support your child's developmental needs while fostering creativity, independence, and joy. We believe that learning happens through both play and structure, and our staff is committed to delivering a program that is enriching as it is nurturing. Whether your child is just beginning their experience or preparing for school, we are here to support, encourage, and inspire them every step of the way. Thank you for trusting us to be a part of your child's learning journey.
